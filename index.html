Rules

  • The aim of the game is to click all the number in the ascending order.
  • Numbers from 1 to 4,5,6.. are spawned randomly in a 5*5 grid.
  • The numbers appear for a few seconds.
  • You have to click the numbers from 1 to n in the ascending order.
  • HighScore is the highest n you succeeded where you click all the numbers from 1 to n succesfully.
Rules

  • The aim of the game is to flip/match all the cards.
  • All the cards will be randomly spawned in the 4*4 grid upside down.
  • When you click a card it flips.
  • You can match cards if you click 2 same cards one after the other.
  • When 2 same cards are clicked they stay fliped else they turn upside down.
  • HighScore the time taken to complete the grid
